About the Artist

My work often teeters on the edge between humor and social critique. Inspired by the absurd and sometimes ridiculous sides of the world, I create concepts that play with both the digital and the tactile. With a background in Design, Art, and Technology, my multidisciplinary practice exists of video, animation, 3D modeling, 3D scanning, installations and whatever fits the concept for example ; pictures of meat.
