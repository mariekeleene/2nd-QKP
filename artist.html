About the Artist

My work often teeters on the edge between humor and social critique. Inspired by the absurd and sometimes ridiculous sides of the world, I create concepts that play with both the digital and the tactile. With a background in Design, Art, and Technology, my multidisciplinary practice blends digital animations with materials like textiles and ceramics.

I aim for laughter, but the kind that lingers—the sort that only comes when the underlying absurdity truly sinks in. Through a poetic and playful approach, I tackle serious themes, often with a touch of mockery or sharp wit, while never losing the joy of simply creating something fun.
