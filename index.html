About the Artist ▼

My work explores the strange and often humorous contradictions of living in a world shaped by technology, global systems, and environmental crises. Working across video, installation, performance, sound, and material experimentation, I create situations where digital logic collides with physical reality.

I am particularly interested in translation: how things change when they move between worlds — from physical to digital, from data to material, from machine perception to human interpretation. In these moments of translation, something is always lost, distorted, or unexpectedly revealed.
