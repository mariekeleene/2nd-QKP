About the Artist ▼

Marieke Leene based in PT(aveiro) working with humor, societal critique Concepts take precedence in the creative process, as I select materials based on the idea I wish to convey. Her work often addresses political or societal issues, using humor and wit to present critical ideas in a poetic and colorful way. Marieke also integrates AI into her work, that was mostly in its earlier, more primitive stages, which offered unique challenges and opportunities for exploration. Recent projects include larger outdoor installations, such as a fountain for an exhibition in Lagos and a light installation and a big textile/plastic quilt for a biennial in Luxembourg..
