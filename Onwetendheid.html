Free Embroidery — 2024

15 × 15 cm
Made by Maria Virginia de Almeida Aguiar

This hand-embroidered work was created in 2024 and draws inspiration from the practice of Dutch artist Marieke Leene, particularly her installation presented at the ArtEZ Finals 2022 in Arnhem.

Through embroidery, Maria Virginia translates this critical perspective into textile form, connecting contemporary artistic discourse with traditional handcraft.

Her practice is rooted in Brazilian embroidery traditions and Indigenous artisanship, weaving together ancestral knowledge and contemporary art.
