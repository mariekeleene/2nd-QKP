Title: And It Might Not Be True

Year: 2025

Medium: text/ filmstills

Showed at: Layer Gallery Bucharest

This work reflects on misinformation and the nature of reality. To understand what is going wrong, and to trace where misinformation begins, we must be critical not only of others but, most importantly, of ourselves. We can easily fall into thinking traps — often without realizing it — and terms and concepts can feel vague or difficult. Yet it is our responsibility to seek clarity.
