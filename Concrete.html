Title: Concrete Lover

Year: 2025

Medium: text/ film/ photo

Showed at: Jelsa Art Biennial 2025/ Concrete Tenderness

Concrete lover was a reflective work created during a 4 month residency on the island Hvar in Croatia, hosted by LAB852. The work shows a love for concrete that came to bloom after being baptised by an environment full of concrete. the work questions the qualities of this seemingly beautiful material by a metaphorical text. The video created with the work showed an island slowely drowning in its most beloved material.
